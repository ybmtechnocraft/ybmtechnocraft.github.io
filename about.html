About Us

About YBM Techno Craft

YBM Techno Craft was founded in 2008 by Mr. Malappa, a highly skilled mold design engineer with over 27 years of experience. Our company specializes in precision-engineered mold solutions, ensuring durability, cost-effectiveness, and high-quality standards for various industries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, precision-engineered mold solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients. We aim to deliver durable and cost-effective molds that support efficient production processes and drive the success of our customers in various industries.

Our Vision

To be one of the most preferred suppliers to our customers, from concept to delivery, in the development of tools & value-added services, with innovative and sound manufacturing solutions, in the chosen markets on a global platform.

  • Innovate And Adopt New Technologies
  • Focus On Sustainability
  • Improve Cost Efficiency

  • Achieve Operational Excellence
