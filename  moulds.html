Moulds

Stack Mould

Stack moulds are an advanced form of multi-level injection moulding technology that significantly increases production efficiency. This 8+8 cavity stack mould design allows for the simultaneous production of two identical sets of parts per cycle, effectively doubling the output compared to conventional single-face moulds. Key features of this stack mould include an aggressive cycle time of just 12 seconds, thin-walled part capability, stripper ejection for seamless part removal, and a sophisticated hot runner system that ensures uniform melt flow and reduces material waste. Additionally, a custom-made rack and gear mechanism optimizes the performance and longevity of the mould.

Bi-Material Mould

The Bi-Material mould enables the integration of two distinct materials within a single injection moulding process. This moulding technique is particularly useful for products that require a combination of rigid and flexible materials, such as razor handles. The innovative design combines a rigid inner core with a soft-touch outer layer, enhancing grip, durability, and overall functionality. By utilizing a precise overmoulding process, this technology ensures that the two materials bond seamlessly, providing superior mechanical properties and aesthetic appeal.

Bi-Color Mould

Bi-Color moulds are designed to facilitate the injection of two different colors into a single component, without the need for secondary processing. This is particularly advantageous for applications such as SWR seal pipes, where a rigid outer layer is required for structural strength, while the inner layer remains flexible to provide a secure and leak-proof seal. The precision of this moulding process ensures uniform color distribution and material bonding, making it ideal for consumer goods, automotive parts, and industrial applications.

LSR Mould

Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) moulding represents a breakthrough in high-precision moulding technology. YBM Techno Craft pioneered the development of LSR moulds in India, exhibiting this innovation at Plast India Exhibition 2023. LSR moulds are widely used in medical, automotive, and consumer product applications due to their exceptional durability, flexibility, and resistance to extreme temperatures. The LSR injection process allows for intricate part designs, superior biocompatibility, and enhanced chemical resistance, making it an ideal choice for manufacturing high-quality silicone products.

Collapsible Core

Collapsible core moulds are an advanced solution for manufacturing complex hollow parts with undercuts, which are otherwise difficult to eject using conventional moulds. YBM Techno Craft developed a collapsible core system with side-core sweep functionality, specifically designed for pipe applications. This technology enhances precision in demoulding operations, minimizes cycle time, and ensures consistent quality. The successful export of this mould design to Saudi Arabia showcases its global reliability and effectiveness.

Unscrewing Moulds

Unscrewing moulds are specially designed for the production of threaded plastic parts that require automatic unscrewing during the demoulding process. These moulds use mechanical or hydraulic rotation systems to efficiently remove threaded components without damaging the part’s integrity. Ideal for high-precision applications such as bottle caps, connectors, and closures, unscrewing moulds ensure fast, automated, and reliable production cycles, eliminating the need for manual interventions.
